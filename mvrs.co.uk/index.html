What is a racking inspection?

Under the Provision and Use of Work Equipment Regulations 1998 (PUWER), pallet racking is classed as work equipment and is therefore subject to inspection. The warehouse racking inspection is an HSE racking legislative review that must form part of your risk assessment and happen annually, at a minimum. Marked on a traffic light system.
